Complete a single unfinished story.

Overview

You will be shown an incomplete three-part story. Your task is to complete the story and describe its central message / theme.

Instructions

  1. Review an incomplete three-part story. The beginning and the ending of the story are given.
  2. Complete the short story by writing a probable middle of the story that explains why the ending follows after the beginning.
  3. Justify your explanation: author a generic central message / rule of thumb that this story conveys. Imagine you are explaining this to a child.

Examples

Tips and Rules

  1. General
    • Input sentences must be short and have fewer than 10 words.
    • Input sentences must be simple, as if narrating to a child.
  2. Authoring the middle
    • Avoid introducing any extraneous information.
    • Use names instead of pronouns (e.g. he / she) wherever possible.
  3. Central Message / Rule of Thumb
    • Must be expressed as a general rule of thumb.
           For example: people don't like wearing soiled clothes.
